Master of Salem

GameDesign LevelDesign Scripting
Master of Salem is a 3D puzzle game that I made with a friend as final-project for my studies at Ludus academy. The project was highly praised for its originality and quality.

In Master of Salem, you are sent to the home of Ms. Osborne, accused of witchcraft, to find out if she is a real witch or not. Once arrived, you are trapped in a weird dimension where every wall is misplaced. Use your wand to move the walls and find your way out! But beware, walls are not your only enemies, gravity too...
  • • Designed 9 levels and 1 hub
  • • Creation of a Level Manager script
  • • Level decoration
  • • Puzzle mechanic
  • • Debugging/optimisation
